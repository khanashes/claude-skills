---
name: adr
description: Draft an Architecture Decision Record for a technical decision. Use when user is deciding between approaches or wants to document a decision.
---
Draft an ADR (Architecture Decision Record) in this format:

**Title:** ADR-NNN: [Short noun phrase]
**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded
**Date:** [today]

**Context**
What is the situation forcing this decision? Include constraints, current state, and why the status quo is insufficient.

**Decision Drivers**
Bullet list of the key criteria (performance, simplicity, team familiarity, cost, operational burden).

**Considered Options**
For each option (minimum 3):
- What it is
- Pros
- Cons
- Why it was or wasn't chosen

**Decision**
State the chosen option and the primary reason.

**Consequences**
Positive consequences, negative consequences, risks, and what this decision forecloses.

**Implementation Notes**
Concrete next steps to enact the decision.

Ask clarifying questions about constraints and alternatives before drafting if not enough context is given.
